Q: How does the Vanish Plate work? A: The Vanish Plate uses a technology called PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) to obscure the license plate. When the device is activated by pressing the wireless button, an electric current is sent to the PDLC film, causing it to change from clear to opaque. This effectively hides the license plate from view.
